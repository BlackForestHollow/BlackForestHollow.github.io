Perpetuforest

A procedurally generated survival-horror game

About the Game

In Perpetuforest, players are trapped in a cursed, ever-shifting forest filled with secrets, monsters, and unspeakable horrors. Every wrong choice resets the nightmare, and each reset escalates the terror.

Features

  • Procedurally generated environments
  • Persistent clue journal and puzzle solving
  • Dynamic pathing with escalating consequences
  • Unreal Engine 5 visuals with low-poly horror aesthetic
  • Atmospheric sound and unpredictable monster AI
